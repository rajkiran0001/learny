Terms and Conditions

1. Scope

The following General Terms and Conditions (hereinafter referred to as “GTC”) apply to the booking of and participation in courses (hereinafter referred to as “course”) at rtraining The Learning Hub by the course participant. The terms and conditions of rtraining The Learning Hub apply exclusively. Any terms and conditions to the contrary are hereby expressly contradicted.

2. Conditions of participation for language courses

2.1 Currently offered courses including scope, content, dates, times, etc can be seen on the website or discussed by contacting us.

2.2 We offer both free and paid courses. A paid courses (A1, A2 etc. or beginner, intermediate, etc) comprises 5 to 10 hours each.

2.3 One lesson is 45 to 60 minutes.

2.4 The minimum age to participate in a course is 5.

2.5 Courses are offered regularly on Friday, Saturday and Sunday. There could be classes on public holidays. Please check the schedule. The lessons missed as a result will not be made up at a later date, however if possible you can discuss about it with the teacher.

2.6 The maximum number of participants in a course is ten, the minimum number of participants is one.

2.7 If the teacher has not shown up during the class period or within 10 minutes after the class started, the money will be returned or the class will be postponed. The registered participants will be informed as soon as possible. The paid money will not be refunded, however you can reschedule the classes for later time after taking to the teacher.

2.8 In the event of a sickness or illness of the teacher, the classes will be postponed. We will inform you 1 hour before the start of the class.

3. Registration and reservation

3.1 Registration for a course must be made by contacting Erbee The Learning Hub via Whatapp, email or submitting the contact form in the website.

3.2 After registering for the course, Erbee The Learning Hub will discuss the necessary details with the participant. Once these have been settled, the participant will receive a confirmation of registration.

3.3.With confirmation of registration, the course is reserved for the participant.

3.4 With confirmation of registration and invoice, the course is reserved for the participant for a maximum of seven days. During this time, payment of the course fee must be made in accordance with section 4.1. If no payment is made, the reservation is forfeited and rtraining Language School may give the place to another interested party. rtraining Language School is obligated to conduct the course only if the course fee has been received.

3.5 Online Registration is possible.

3.6 rtraining The Learning Hub reserves the right to schedule a vacation week. This will be communicated to the participants before they register for the course.

4. Payment of the course fee

4.1 Payment can be done by bank transfer to an account, paypal, credit or debit card specified by rtraining The Learning Hub. Possible fees for foreign bank transfers are not covered by rtraining The Learning Hub and will be charged separately to the participant.

4.2 Course and workbooks are not included in the course fees. The placement test and other materials are free of charge.

5. Interruptions

5.1 An interruption of the booked course is usually not possible. However, in exceptional cases, an interruption may be requested and approved via email.

5.2 In the event of (partial) non-participation in a booked course, there is no entitlement to (partial) reimbursement of the money paid.

6. Postponement and cancellation

6.1 If participants are unable to attend the booked course, this must be communicated to rtraining The Learning Hub via email.

6.2 In case of postponement or cancellation of a booked course, the money will not be refunded, however you can reschedule the classes for future dates.

6.3.The course fee agreed upon at the time of registration and paid money is valid for a maximum of one year. In case of postponement to a later date, it is free of charge.

6.4 rtraining The Learning Hub is not a Telc, Goethe or Test-Daf examination center and is therefore not authorized to administer these specific examinations or issue certificates.

7. Placement test

7.1 Beforehand, If the participants needed they can take a placement test to determine their language level and join the classes.

8.3 The participants can object to the use of their data at any time by sending an objection by e-mail to info.rtraining@gmail.com
