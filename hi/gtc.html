Terms and Conditions

1. Scope

The following General Terms and Conditions (hereinafter referred to as “GTC”) apply to the booking of and participation in courses at rteaching The Learning Hub by the course participants. The terms and conditions of rteaching The Learning Hub apply exclusively. Any terms and conditions to the contrary are hereby expressly contradicted.

2. Conditions of participation for language courses

2.1 Currently offered courses including scope, content, dates, times, etc can be seen on the website or discussed by contacting us.

2.2 We offer both free and paid courses. A paid courses (A1, A2 etc. or beginner, intermediate, etc) comprises 5 to 10 hours each.

2.3 One lesson is 45 to 60 minutes.

2.4 The minimum age to participate in a course is 5.

2.5 Courses are offered regularly. Please check the course plan or contact us anytime. We send response as soon as possible within 24 hours. There could be classes on public holidays. Please check the schedule. The lessons missed as a result may be made up at a later date, depending upon the Tutor and the coincidence of the classes

2.6 The maximum number of participants in a course is 10, the minimum number of participants is 1.

2.7 If the Tutor has not shown up during the class period or within 10 minutes after the class started, the money will be returned or the class will be postponed. The registered participants will be informed as soon as possible. The paid money will not be refunded, however you can reschedule the classes for later time after taking to the Tutor.

2.8 In the event of a sickness or illness of the Tutor, the classes will be postponed. We will inform you as soon as possible before 1 hour to the start of the class.

3. Registration and reservation

3.1 Registration for a course must be made by contacting rteaching The Learning Hub via Whatsapp, email for submitting the contact form in the website.

3.2 After registering to the course, rteaching The Learning Hub will discuss the necessary details with the participants. Once these have been settled, the participants will receive a confirmation of registration and further steps to join the courses.

3.3 With the confirmation of registration, the course is reserved for the participants.

3.4 The course is reserved for the participants for a maximum of seven days as soon as the participants receive the confirmation mail. During this time, payment of the course fee can be made in accordance with section 4.1. If the participants are not shown up during the course time, the reservation is forfeited and rteaching the learning hub may give the place to another interested party.

3.5 Online Registration is possible.

3.6 rteaching The Learning Hub reserves the right to schedule a vacation week. This will be communicated to the participants before they register for the course.

4. Payment of the course

4.1 Payment can be done by bank transfer to an account, paypal, credit or debit card specified by rteaching The Learning Hub. Possible fees for foreign bank transfers are not covered by rteaching The Learning Hub

4.2 Course and workbooks are not included in the course fees. Course materials and other materials are free of charge.

5. Interruptions

5.1 An interruption of the booked course is usually not possible. However, in exceptional cases, an interruption may be requested and approved via email.

5.2 In the event of (partial) non-participation in a booked course, the participants can request rteaching The Learning Hub to attend the course at a later time.

6. Postponement and cancellation

6.1 If participants are unable to attend the booked course, this must be communicated to rteaching The Learning Hub.

6.2 The money sent to rteaching The Learning Hub is non refundable

6.3 The money sent to rteaching The Learning Hub is valid for a maximum of one year. In this time you can reschedule to course later upon request and availability.

6.4 rteaching The Learning Hub is not a Telc, Goethe or Test-Daf examination center and is therefore not authorized to administer these specific examinations or issue certificates.

7.1 If the participants needed, they can take a placement test to determine their language level and join the classes. In this case, please contact us beforehand
