structure

once you have in contact with us, you will be given a 10 minites test to access your level. Then you will be contacted to attend the available courses. Each term there is a good number of events where you can further improve your Skills. use methods which promote natural learning for beginners starting, as a child might, with listening and speaking which then naturally progress to the written word. This approach differs from conventional methods, where teaching reading and writing skills first can often leave students with little confidence and ability to communicate and use their new language in the real world. Not only is our way a successful means to start speaking a foreign language, learning can be a lot of fun, too.
